Taco Recipe

My family always looks forward to our weekly taco night, this recipe has been handed down from my great grandfather from Norway it's always served us well and I hope it will do the same for you!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of Flank Steak
  • 1 Onion
  • Juice of 1 Lime
  • 1/4 Cupe of Cilantro
  • Salt to taste
  • 8 Corn Tortillas

Steps

  1. Salt steak liberally before grilling for 4 minutes on each side
  2. While steak rests, dice your onion and chop cilantro
  3. Slice steak into 1/4 inch strips
  4. Heat tortillas on a pan until they are warm and pliable
  5. Fill tortilla with a few slices of steak, raw onion, a pinch of cilantro and a squeeze of lime
  6. Feek free to add any hot sauce you love, if you are feeling spicy
