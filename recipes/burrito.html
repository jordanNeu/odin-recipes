Burrito Recipe!

Burrito's are extremely similar to tacos, the main difference being the size and that they are enclosed. The seal on a burrito is what makes or breaks the experience. I'm going to tell you how to load a burrito perfectly and keep it closed.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb of Ground Beef
  • 1/2 Onion
  • 1 Medium Tomato/li>
  • 1 Can of Black Beans
  • 1 Cup of Shredded Lettuce
  • 1/2 Cup of Shredded Cheese
  • 3 Tbsp of Sour Cream
  • 4 Flour Tortillas

Steps

  1. Brown ground beef and season to your preference
  2. While beef is browning, heat black beans and salt lightly
  3. Dice onion and tomato and set aside for garnish
  4. Heat tortilla in a pan until is is pliable
  5. Spread sour cream onto tortilla, top with beef, black beans, tomatoes, onions into the center of the flattened tortilla
  6. Top with shredded lettuce and cheese
  7. Fold the left and right sides into the middle of the tortilla, then take the side closest to you and folded it over the middle
  8. Tightly but gently pull the mass towards you being sure to keep the filling contained, then fold the final flap over the burrito
  9. Drop the burrito, flap-side down onto a warm pan and lightly toast until the flap has sealed
