Taco Recipe!

The quesadilla is synonomous with comfort, it's like a grilled cheese but with more possibilities. Its flaky, gooey, warm and wonderful. Just like all of my readers!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup of Shredded Cheese
  • 2 Tbsp Butter
  • 2 Large Flour Tortillas

Steps

  1. Warm a 10-inch pan over medium-hight heat
  2. Drop in a tbsp of butter, moving around the pan til melted
  3. Lay down the tortilla, dropping in half of your shreded cheese shortly after
  4. Once cheese has begun to melt slighty, fold the tortilla in half
  5. Let quesadilla crisp on one side for 3-4 minutes before turning over
  6. Once the other side has crisped, remove from pan and slice and half
  7. Plate and enjoy!
